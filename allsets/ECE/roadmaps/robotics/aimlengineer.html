AI/Machine Learning Engineer (Robotics)

AI/ML Engineers in robotics design, implement, and optimize algorithms that enable robots to learn, adapt, and make decisions. They work on perception, planning, control, and reinforcement learning for autonomous systems.
A typical day involves coding, training models, simulation, and deploying solutions on hardware.

Learn more >

  1. Mathematics & Statistics Build a strong base in mathematics, probability, and statistics for ML algorithms.
  2. Programming Skills Master Python, C++, and ML libraries (TensorFlow, PyTorch, scikit-learn).
  3. Machine Learning Fundamentals Learn supervised, unsupervised, and reinforcement learning techniques.
  4. Deep Learning & Neural Networks Study CNNs, RNNs, and advanced neural architectures for robotics.
  5. Computer Vision & Perception Integrate vision and sensor data for robot perception.
  6. Planning & Decision Making Develop algorithms for path planning, decision making, and control.
  7. Simulation & Testing Use simulation tools and datasets for training and testing ML models.
  8. Deployment on Robotics Hardware Optimize and deploy ML models on embedded and real-time systems.
  9. Collaboration & Documentation Work with cross-functional teams and document your work.
  10. Soft Skills Communication, teamwork, and project management for successful delivery.
