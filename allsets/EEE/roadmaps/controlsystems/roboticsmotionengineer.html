Robotics & Motion Engineer

Robotics & Motion Engineers design, implement, and maintain robotics and motion control solutions for manufacturing, processing, and energy conversion. They focus on efficiency, reliability, and innovation in industrial environments.
A typical day involves system design, programming, troubleshooting, and collaboration with manufacturing and process teams.

Learn more >

  1. Electrical & Automation Fundamentals Build a strong foundation in electrical circuits, automation principles, and materials.
  2. Robotics & Motion Devices Learn about sensors, actuators, PLCs, SCADA, and industrial protocols for robotics and motion control.
  3. Motion Control Programming Study programming languages and frameworks for motion control (PLC, C++, Python).
  4. Control Strategies Explore PID, advanced control algorithms, and real-time control systems.
  5. Integration & Commissioning Integrate robotics and motion solutions and commission systems for operation.
  6. Process Optimization Analyze and optimize automated processes for efficiency and reliability.
  7. Safety & Standards Work with safety standards (ISO, IEC) and ensure safe operation of robotics and motion systems.
  8. Troubleshooting & Maintenance Troubleshoot and maintain robotics and motion systems.
  9. Documentation & Reporting Document robotics and motion designs, maintenance, and process improvements.
  10. Soft Skills Communication, teamwork, and project management for successful delivery.
