Power Electronics Design Engineer

Power Electronics Design Engineers design, implement, and maintain power electronic circuits and systems for industrial, renewable, and consumer applications. They focus on efficiency, reliability, and innovation in energy conversion.
A typical day involves circuit design, simulation, troubleshooting, and collaboration with manufacturing and energy teams.

Learn more >

  1. Electrical & Electronics Fundamentals Build a strong foundation in electrical circuits, semiconductors, and materials.
  2. Power Electronic Devices Learn about diodes, transistors, thyristors, IGBTs, and MOSFETs.
  3. Converter & Inverter Design Study topologies, design, and control of converters and inverters.
  4. Simulation & Modeling Use simulation tools (MATLAB, PSpice) for design and analysis.
  5. Thermal Management & Protection Design cooling systems and protection schemes for power electronics.
  6. Testing & Commissioning Test, commission, and troubleshoot power electronic systems.
  7. Integration & Application Integrate power electronics in renewable, industrial, and consumer systems.
  8. Safety & Standards Work with safety standards (IEC, IEEE) and ensure safe operation of power electronics.
  9. Documentation & Reporting Document designs, maintenance, and process improvements.
  10. Soft Skills Communication, teamwork, and project management for successful delivery.
