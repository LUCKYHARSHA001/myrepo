Simulation & Testing Engineer

Simulation & Testing Engineers design, implement, and validate systems using simulation tools and testing methodologies for industrial, renewable, and consumer applications. They focus on efficiency, reliability, and innovation in design validation.
A typical day involves simulation, testing, troubleshooting, and collaboration with design and manufacturing teams.

Learn more >

  1. Electrical & Electronics Fundamentals Build a strong foundation in electrical circuits, semiconductors, and materials.
  2. Simulation Tools & Techniques Learn about simulation tools (MATLAB, PSpice, Simulink) and techniques for design validation.
  3. Testing Methodologies Study testing methods, standards, and protocols for power electronics and automation.
  4. Design Validation & Verification Validate and verify designs using simulation and testing results.
  5. Thermal Management & Protection Design cooling systems and protection schemes for tested systems.
  6. Integration & Application Integrate simulation and testing in renewable, industrial, and consumer systems.
  7. Safety & Standards Work with safety standards (IEC, IEEE) and ensure safe operation of tested systems.
  8. Documentation & Reporting Document simulation and testing results, maintenance, and process improvements.
  9. Soft Skills Communication, teamwork, and project management for successful delivery.
