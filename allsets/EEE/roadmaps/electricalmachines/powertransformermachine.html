Power Transformer Machine Engineer

Power Transformer Machine Engineers design, analyze, and maintain power transformers for electrical machines and power systems. They work on insulation, cooling, protection, and testing to ensure reliable operation.
A typical day involves transformer design, simulation, testing, troubleshooting, and collaboration with utilities and manufacturers.
