SCADA Engineer

SCADA Engineers design, implement, and maintain SCADA systems for industrial processes and automation. They focus on reliability, efficiency, and safety in automated environments.
A typical day involves system design, PLC/SCADA programming, troubleshooting, and collaboration with manufacturing and process teams.

Learn more >

  1. Mathematics & Control Theory Build a strong foundation in mathematics, control theory, and system dynamics.
  2. Automation & SCADA Fundamentals Learn about automation principles, SCADA architecture, sensors, actuators, and industrial protocols.
  3. PLC & SCADA Systems Study PLC programming, SCADA systems, and HMI design for industrial automation.
  4. Control Strategies Explore PID, advanced control algorithms, and real-time control systems.
  5. Integration & Commissioning Integrate SCADA solutions and commission systems for operation.
  6. Process Optimization Analyze and optimize automated processes for efficiency and reliability.
  7. Safety & Standards Work with safety standards (ISO, IEC) and ensure safe operation of SCADA systems.
  8. Troubleshooting & Maintenance Troubleshoot and maintain SCADA systems.
  9. Documentation & Reporting Document SCADA designs, maintenance, and process improvements.
  10. Soft Skills Communication, teamwork, and project management for successful delivery.
