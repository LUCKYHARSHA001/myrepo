Foundation Engineer

A Foundation Engineer specializes in the design, analysis, and construction of foundations for buildings, bridges, and other structures. They ensure stability, safety, and performance by considering soil conditions and loads.
A typical day involves site investigation, geotechnical analysis, foundation design, construction supervision, and collaboration with project teams.
