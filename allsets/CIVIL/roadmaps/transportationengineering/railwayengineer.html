Railway Engineer

A Railway Engineer plans, designs, and manages the construction and maintenance of railway tracks, stations, and signaling systems. They ensure safety, efficiency, and reliability of rail transportation networks.
A typical day involves track alignment, signaling design, rolling stock selection, site supervision, and collaboration with agencies and contractors.

  2. Track Alignment & Design Design track alignments, profiles, and cross-sections for safety and efficiency.

  7. Construction Management Plan, supervise, and manage railway construction activities.

  9. Project Management Plan, schedule, and manage railway projects and budgets.
  10. Collaboration & Communication Work with agencies, communities, and stakeholders for project delivery.
