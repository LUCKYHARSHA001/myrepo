Transportation Planner

A Transportation Planner develops strategies, policies, and plans for efficient, sustainable, and equitable transportation systems. They analyze travel demand, forecast growth, and coordinate with agencies and communities.
A typical day involves data analysis, modeling, stakeholder engagement, policy development, and project management.

Learn more >

  1. Transportation Planning Fundamentals Learn about planning principles, travel demand, and system design.
  2. Data Collection & Analysis Collect and analyze travel data, surveys, and demographic information.
  3. Travel Demand Modeling Use modeling tools (TransCAD, VISUM, CUBE) to forecast travel patterns.
  4. Public Transport Planning Plan and optimize bus, rail, and other public transport systems.
  5. Non-Motorized Transport Promote walking, cycling, and other sustainable transport modes.
  6. Policy & Regulatory Framework Develop policies, regulations, and standards for transportation systems.
  7. Environmental & Social Impact Assessment Assess and mitigate impacts of transportation projects.
  8. Stakeholder Engagement Coordinate with agencies, communities, and stakeholders for project success.
  9. Project Management Plan, schedule, and manage transportation planning projects and budgets.
  10. Collaboration & Communication Work with agencies, communities, and stakeholders for project delivery.
