Heat Transfer Engineer

A Heat Transfer Engineer specializes in the study and application of conduction, convection, and radiation to solve engineering problems. They design heat exchangers, insulation, and thermal management systems for industries such as energy, automotive, and manufacturing.
A typical day involves analyzing heat flow, designing efficient exchangers, and troubleshooting thermal issues in complex systems.
