HVAC Engineer

An HVAC Engineer specializes in the design, analysis, and optimization of heating, ventilation, air conditioning, and refrigeration systems. They work on residential, commercial, and industrial projects to ensure comfort, efficiency, and sustainability.
A typical day involves designing HVAC layouts, performing load calculations, selecting equipment, and troubleshooting system issues.
