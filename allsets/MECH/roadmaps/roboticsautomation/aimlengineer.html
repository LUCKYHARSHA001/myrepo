AI & Machine Learning Engineer

An AI & Machine Learning Engineer specializes in designing, developing, and deploying intelligent algorithms and models for robotics, automation, and IT. They work with data, software, and hardware to create adaptive, data-driven systems.
A typical day involves building ML models, training neural networks, integrating AI with automation, and collaborating with cross-functional teams.

Learn more >

  1. AI & ML Fundamentals Master principles of artificial intelligence, supervised, unsupervised, and reinforcement learning.
  2. Mathematical Foundations Apply statistics, linear algebra, calculus, and probability for ML algorithms.
  3. Programming & Frameworks Use Python, TensorFlow, PyTorch, or scikit-learn for model development.
  4. Data Collection & Preprocessing Gather, clean, and preprocess data for training and evaluation.
  5. Model Training & Evaluation Train, validate, and tune ML models for accuracy and performance.
  6. Deep Learning & Neural Networks Build and deploy deep learning models for advanced AI applications.
  7. Deployment & Integration Deploy AI/ML models in robotics, automation, and IT systems.
  8. Industry Applications Apply AI/ML in robotics, manufacturing, healthcare, and IT.
  9. Collaboration & Documentation Communicate findings, write technical reports, and collaborate with teams.
