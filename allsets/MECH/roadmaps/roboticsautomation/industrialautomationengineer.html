Industrial Automation Engineer

An Industrial Automation Engineer specializes in designing, programming, and optimizing automated systems for manufacturing, robotics, and process industries. They work with PLCs, SCADA, sensors, actuators, and software to improve efficiency, safety, and reliability.
A typical day involves programming control systems, integrating hardware, troubleshooting automation issues, and collaborating with cross-functional teams.
