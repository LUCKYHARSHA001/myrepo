Electric Vehicle Engineer

An Electric Vehicle Engineer specializes in the design, analysis, and optimization of electric propulsion systems, battery packs, and charging infrastructure. They work on improving vehicle range, efficiency, and sustainability for cars, trucks, and other EVs.
A typical day involves modeling battery systems, integrating electric motors, and troubleshooting EV performance issues.

  2. Battery Technology & Management Study battery chemistries, BMS, and charging systems for EVs.
