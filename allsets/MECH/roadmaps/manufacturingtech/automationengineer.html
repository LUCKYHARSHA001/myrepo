Automation Engineer

An Automation Engineer specializes in the design, implementation, and optimization of automated systems and robotics for manufacturing. They work on process control, PLC programming, and integration of smart technologies for efficient production.
A typical day involves programming PLCs, troubleshooting automation systems, and optimizing workflows for productivity.
