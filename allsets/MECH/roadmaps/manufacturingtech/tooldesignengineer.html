Tool Design Engineer

A Tool Design Engineer specializes in the design, modeling, and optimization of tools, dies, jigs, and fixtures for manufacturing. They work on improving production efficiency, quality, and cost-effectiveness for various industries.
A typical day involves creating CAD models, selecting materials, and troubleshooting tool-related issues.
