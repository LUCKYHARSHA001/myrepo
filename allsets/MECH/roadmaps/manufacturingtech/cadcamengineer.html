CAD/CAM Engineer

A CAD/CAM Engineer specializes in the use of computer-aided design and manufacturing software to create, analyze, and optimize products and processes. They work on 2D/3D modeling, CNC programming, and digital manufacturing for various industries.
A typical day involves creating CAD models, generating CAM toolpaths, and troubleshooting design-to-manufacturing workflows.
