Incident Responder

The Incident Responder role focuses on identifying, analyzing, and mitigating security incidents. These professionals react quickly to threats, contain breaches, investigate causes, and restore operations. Their work combines rapid decision-making, technical skills, and strong coordination to defend digital assets.
