Cybersecurity Consultant

The Cybersecurity Consultant role involves advising organizations on how to protect their IT infrastructure from cyber risks. Consultants assess vulnerabilities, design security strategies, implement best practices, and ensure regulatory compliance. Their work bridges technical insight with risk management expertise.
