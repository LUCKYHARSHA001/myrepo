Penetration Tester

The Penetration Tester, or ethical hacker, simulates cyberattacks on systems, applications, and networks to identify security vulnerabilities. They use tools and techniques similar to malicious hackers but do so legally to help organizations strengthen their defenses.
They play a crucial role in proactively identifying weaknesses before attackers can exploit them.

  1. WiresharkCaptures and inspects network packets to detect threats, analyze traffic, and troubleshoot communication issues.
  2. SplunkAnalyzes system logs to monitor activities, detect threats, and provide security alerts in real time.
  3. NmapScans networks to find open ports, services, and potential vulnerabilities in connected devices.
  4. Burp SuiteTests web applications by intercepting and modifying HTTP requests to uncover security flaws.
  5. SIEMCollects and correlates logs from systems to detect threats and support incident response.
