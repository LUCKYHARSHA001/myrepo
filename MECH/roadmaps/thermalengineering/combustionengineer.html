Combustion Engineer

A Combustion Engineer specializes in the study and application of chemical reactions, thermodynamics, and fluid mechanics to design and optimize combustion systems. They work on engines, turbines, furnaces, and environmental controls for industries such as automotive, aerospace, and energy.
A typical day involves analyzing combustion processes, designing burners, and troubleshooting emissions and efficiency issues.
