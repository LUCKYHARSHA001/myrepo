Refrigeration Engineer

A Refrigeration Engineer specializes in the design, analysis, and optimization of refrigeration and cooling systems. They work on industrial, commercial, and residential projects to ensure efficient thermal management and energy utilization.
A typical day involves designing refrigeration cycles, selecting equipment, performing load calculations, and troubleshooting system issues.
