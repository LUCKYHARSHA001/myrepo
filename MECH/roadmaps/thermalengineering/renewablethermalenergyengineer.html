Renewable Thermal Energy Engineer

A Renewable Thermal Energy Engineer specializes in the design, analysis, and optimization of systems that harness renewable sources for heating and cooling. They work on solar thermal, geothermal, biomass, and other sustainable technologies to improve energy efficiency and reduce environmental impact.
A typical day involves designing solar collectors, modeling geothermal systems, and integrating renewable solutions into buildings and industry.
