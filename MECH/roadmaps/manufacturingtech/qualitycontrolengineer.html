Quality Control Engineer

A Quality Control Engineer specializes in the development, implementation, and optimization of quality control systems and standards. They work on process audits, defect analysis, and continuous improvement for manufacturing.
A typical day involves inspecting products, analyzing data, and troubleshooting quality issues.
