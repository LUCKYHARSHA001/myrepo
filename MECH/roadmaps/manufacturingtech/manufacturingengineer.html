Manufacturing Engineer

A Manufacturing Engineer specializes in the design, analysis, and optimization of manufacturing processes for products and components. They work on process planning, automation, quality assurance, and lean manufacturing for various industries.
A typical day involves developing process flows, implementing automation, and troubleshooting production issues.
