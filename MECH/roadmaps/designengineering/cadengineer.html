CAD Engineer

A CAD Engineer specializes in the use of computer-aided design software to create, analyze, and optimize 2D/3D models for products and components. They work on drafting, modeling, and design documentation for various industries.
A typical day involves creating CAD drawings, collaborating with design teams, and troubleshooting modeling issues.
