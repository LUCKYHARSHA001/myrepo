Fuel Systems Engineer

A Fuel Systems Engineer specializes in the design, analysis, and optimization of fuel delivery, injection, and emissions control systems. They work on improving engine performance, efficiency, and compliance for cars, trucks, and other vehicles.
A typical day involves modeling fuel injection, calibrating control systems, and troubleshooting fuel system issues.
