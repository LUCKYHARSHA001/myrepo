Powertrain Engineer

A Powertrain Engineer specializes in the design, analysis, and optimization of engines, transmissions, drivetrains, and hybrid/electric systems. They work on improving vehicle performance, efficiency, and emissions for cars, trucks, and other vehicles.
A typical day involves modeling engine cycles, calibrating control systems, and troubleshooting powertrain issues.
