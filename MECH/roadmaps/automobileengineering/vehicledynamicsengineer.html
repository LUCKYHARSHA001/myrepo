Vehicle Dynamics Engineer

A Vehicle Dynamics Engineer specializes in the study and optimization of vehicle motion, suspension, steering, and handling. They work on improving ride comfort, stability, and performance for cars, trucks, and motorsport vehicles.
A typical day involves modeling suspension systems, analyzing tire behavior, and tuning vehicle setups for optimal dynamics.
