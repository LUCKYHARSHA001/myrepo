Pneumatics Engineer

A Pneumatics Engineer specializes in the design, analysis, and optimization of systems that use compressed air or gas for automation, control, and power transmission. They work in manufacturing, robotics, automotive, and process industries.
A typical day involves designing pneumatic circuits, selecting components, troubleshooting systems, and collaborating with automation teams.
