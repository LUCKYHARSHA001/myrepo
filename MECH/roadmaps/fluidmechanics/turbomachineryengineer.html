Turbomachinery Engineer

A Turbomachinery Engineer specializes in the design, analysis, and optimization of rotating machines such as turbines, compressors, pumps, and fans. They use simulation tools, experimental methods, and analytical techniques to improve performance, efficiency, and reliability in energy, aerospace, and industrial sectors.
A typical day involves running CFD and FEA simulations, analyzing test data, collaborating with design teams, and troubleshooting turbomachinery issues.
