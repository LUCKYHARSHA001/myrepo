Threat Intelligence Analyst

The Threat Intelligence Analyst role involves gathering, analyzing, and interpreting cyber threat data. Analysts identify threat actors, assess attack trends, and deliver actionable insights to strengthen defenses. Their job blends research, technical tools, and strategic thinking to anticipate cyber risks.
