DevSecOps Engineer

The DevSecOps Engineer role integrates security practices within the DevOps process. They automate security testing, monitor infrastructure, and ensure continuous compliance throughout development and deployment. Their work combines coding, security tools, and collaboration to build secure software pipelines.
