Product Designer

A Product Designer blends UX, UI, strategy, and research to create functional and delightful products. They work closely with cross-functional teams to solve real problems for users while aligning with business goals. The role spans across user research, wireframing, prototyping, visual design, and even some front-end knowledge.

  2. UI DesignCraft intuitive and beautiful interfaces with principles like alignment, hierarchy, and consistency. Learn the visual side of designing for users.
  3. User ResearchLearn how to gather insights through interviews, surveys, and usability tests to understand what users need and how they behave.

  6. AccessibilityEnsure your designs are usable by everyone, including people with disabilities. Learn about contrast, keyboard navigation, and inclusive practices.

  8. Portfolio BuildingShowcase your work, process, and thinking. Craft compelling case studies that highlight your role, challenges faced, and the results achieved.
