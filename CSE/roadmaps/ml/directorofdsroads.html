Director of Data Science

Level: Leadership / Principal

Experience: 15+ Years

A Director of Data Science oversees large-scale data initiatives, aligning machine learning and analytics efforts with business goals.This senior leadership role involves leading teams of data scientists, managing cross-functional collaboration, establishing best practices, and strategic use of data.

Explore responsibilities >

  1. Strategic VisionDefine and communicate long-term data science goals that align with business strategy.
  2. LeadershipManage and mentor teams of data scientists, analysts, and ML engineers across projects.


  3. ML OperationsOversee ML lifecycle management, from model development to deployment and monitoring.
  4. Cross-Department CollaborationCoordinate with engineering, marketing, product, and executive teams to prioritize data initiatives.
  5. Ethics & GovernanceEstablish responsible AI practices and ensure compliance with data regulations and standards.
  6. Stakeholder CommunicationPresent insights and data strategy to executive leadership and external partners.
  7. Technology & ToolsEvaluate and recommend tools, platforms, and methodologies for scalable data science operations.
  8. Budget & Resource ManagementPlan, allocate, and manage data science budgets, infrastructure, and hiring.
