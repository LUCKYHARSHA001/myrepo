Biomass Energy Engineer

Biomass Energy Engineers design, implement, and maintain biomass power systems for residential, commercial, and industrial applications. They focus on efficiency, reliability, and sustainability in renewable energy environments.
A typical day involves system design, installation, troubleshooting, and collaboration with energy and maintenance teams.
