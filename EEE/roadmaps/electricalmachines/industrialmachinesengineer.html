Industrial Machines Engineer

Industrial Machines Engineers design, integrate, and maintain machinery used in manufacturing, processing, and automation. They focus on reliability, efficiency, and safety in industrial environments.
A typical day involves system design, troubleshooting, process optimization, and collaboration with automation and maintenance teams.
