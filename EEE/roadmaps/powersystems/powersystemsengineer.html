Power System Engineer

Power System Engineers design, analyze, and maintain electrical power systems for generation, transmission, and distribution. They work on grid stability, protection, reliability, and integration of renewable energy sources.
A typical day involves system modeling, load flow studies, protection coordination, troubleshooting, and collaboration with utilities and industry partners.
