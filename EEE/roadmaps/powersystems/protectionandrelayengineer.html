Protection & Relay Engineer

Protection & Relay Engineers design, implement, and maintain protection schemes for electrical power systems. They ensure system reliability, safety, and fast fault detection using relays, circuit breakers, and automation.
A typical day involves relay setting calculations, coordination studies, testing, and troubleshooting.
