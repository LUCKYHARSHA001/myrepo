Renewable Energy Engineer

Renewable Energy Engineers design, integrate, and optimize solar, wind, hydro, and other renewable energy systems for power generation and grid integration. They work on system modeling, performance analysis, and sustainability.
A typical day involves system design, simulation, site assessment, and collaboration with utilities and industry partners.
