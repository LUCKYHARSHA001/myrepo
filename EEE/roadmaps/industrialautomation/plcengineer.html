PLC Engineer

PLC Engineers design, implement, and maintain PLC systems for industrial processes and automation. They focus on reliability, efficiency, and safety in automated environments.
A typical day involves system design, PLC programming, troubleshooting, and collaboration with manufacturing and process teams.

Learn more >

  1. Mathematics & Control Theory Build a strong foundation in mathematics, control theory, and system dynamics.
  2. Automation & PLC Fundamentals Learn about automation principles, PLC architecture, sensors, actuators, and industrial protocols.
  3. PLC Programming Study ladder logic, function block, and structured text programming for PLCs.
  4. SCADA & HMI Systems Learn about SCADA integration and HMI design for industrial automation.
  5. Control Strategies Explore PID, advanced control algorithms, and real-time control systems.
  6. Integration & Commissioning Integrate PLC solutions and commission systems for operation.
  7. Process Optimization Analyze and optimize automated processes for efficiency and reliability.
  8. Safety & Standards Work with safety standards (ISO, IEC) and ensure safe operation of PLC systems.
  9. Troubleshooting & Maintenance Troubleshoot and maintain PLC systems.
  10. Documentation & Reporting Document PLC designs, maintenance, and process improvements.
  11. Soft Skills Communication, teamwork, and project management for successful delivery.
