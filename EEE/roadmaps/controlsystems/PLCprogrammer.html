PLC Programmer

PLC Programmers design, implement, and maintain PLC solutions for manufacturing, processing, and energy conversion. They focus on efficiency, reliability, and innovation in industrial environments.
A typical day involves system design, programming, troubleshooting, and collaboration with manufacturing and process teams.
