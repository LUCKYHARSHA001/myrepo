Geotechnical QA/QC Engineer

A Geotechnical QA/QC Engineer specializes in monitoring, testing, and verifying geotechnical works to ensure compliance with standards and specifications. They play a key role in quality management, documentation, and site supervision.
A typical day involves reviewing test results, inspecting construction activities, preparing reports, and coordinating with project teams.

  2. Material Testing & Inspection Conduct soil, rock, and material tests; inspect geotechnical works on site.
  3. Documentation & Reporting Prepare and review test reports, inspection records, and compliance documents.
  4. Site Supervision Monitor construction activities, verify procedures, and ensure adherence to specifications.
  5. Non-Destructive Testing Apply NDT methods for geotechnical structures and foundations.
  6. Quality Management Systems Implement and maintain quality management systems (QMS) for projects.
  7. Health, Safety & Environment Ensure HSE compliance and promote safe practices on site.
  8. Construction Supervision Oversee geotechnical construction, quality control, and safety management.
  9. Project Management Plan, schedule, and manage QA/QC activities and budgets.
  10. Collaboration & Communication Work with engineers, contractors, and stakeholders for project delivery.
