Structural QA/QC Engineer

A Structural QA/QC Engineer is responsible for implementing and monitoring quality assurance and quality control processes in structural engineering projects. They ensure compliance with standards, specifications, and safety requirements.
A typical day involves site inspections, material testing, documentation, reporting, and coordination with project teams.

  2. Codes & Standards Apply relevant codes, standards, and specifications for structural QA/QC.
  3. Material Testing Conduct tests on concrete, steel, and other materials for compliance.
  4. Inspection Techniques Perform site inspections, non-destructive testing, and quality audits.
  5. Documentation & Reporting Prepare QA/QC documentation, test reports, and compliance records.
  6. Root Cause Analysis Investigate failures, defects, and recommend corrective actions.
  7. Site Supervision Monitor construction activities to ensure adherence to quality standards.
  8. Safety & Compliance Ensure safety regulations and compliance with legal requirements.
  9. Continuous Improvement Implement quality improvement initiatives and training programs.
  10. Collaboration & Communication Coordinate with engineers, contractors, and stakeholders for quality delivery.
